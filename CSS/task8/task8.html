Robert Bruce was a famous king of Scotland. He ruled over the country with a good name and fame. His army was defeated 6 times by his enemies. At last, he lost his Kingdom. He fled away to save his life. He took shelter in a cave. He was in a sad mood. One day, the king was lying in the cave and thinking about how he could get back his kingdom. Suddenly, he noticed a spider trying to reach the ceiling of the cave. It failed six times and became successful on the 7th attempt. This sight greatly inspired Robert Bruce. He assembled a formidable army and launched a series of attacks against his enemies. At last, he defeated them and got back his kingdom.
