Texas will unveil its newest state park with a guided hike on New Year’s Day

Alejandra Martinez Dec. 2,2025, 4:36 p.m. Central

Texas is set to unveil its newest state park, the 5,000-acre Palo Pinto Mountains State Park, with a special guided hike on New Year’s Day. The park, located about 70 miles west of Fort Worth, offers stunning views of the Palo Pinto Mountains and a variety of outdoor activities including hiking, bird watching, and camping.

The guided hike will take visitors through some of the park’s most scenic trails, led by experienced park rangers who will share insights about the local flora and fauna. The event is free and open to the public, but space is limited, so early registration is encouraged.
